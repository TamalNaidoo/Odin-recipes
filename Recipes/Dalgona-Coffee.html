Dalgona Coffee (Whipped Coffee)



This viral coffee drink is originally from South Korea and was made famous during the 2020 pandemic


    Ingredients:
  • 2 tablespoons boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 cup whole milk

    Directions:
  1. Whisk boiling water, instant coffee, and sugar together until coffee is dissolved. Continue to whisk until mixture becomes thick and forms a peak, 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Pour milk into a glass. Pour the frothy coffee on top of the milk.
    Recipe Tips:
  • For larger batches, use water, instant coffee, and sugar in a 1:1:1 ratio.
  • You can use any milk of your choice.
  • A mug warmer can keep this hot for longer.
